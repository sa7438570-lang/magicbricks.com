About Bhulekh Pratapgarh

Bhulekh is Uttar Pradesh's official digital land records platform. This website provides Pratapgarh district with comprehensive details on residential and agricultural land, plot characteristics, and ownership status. Users can check their Khatauni (landholding information), download maps of their property, and get their Khasra number (plot identification) using the web portal.

Tehsils in Pratapgarh

  • Kunda
  • Sadar (Pratapgarh)
  • Lalganj Ajhara

How To Check Pratapgarh Land Records Online

Follow these steps to check land records:

Step 1:Go to UP government official website of Bhulekh https://upbhulekh.gov.in

Step 2:Click on the "View Copy of Khatauni" option on the home page.

Step 3:Select your district (Pratapgarh) from the dropdown menu.

Step 4:Choose your tehsil from the available options.

Step 5:Select your village from the list.

Step 6:Enter your Khasra number or other required details to view your land records.

Step 7:View and download your Khatauni (land records) document.
